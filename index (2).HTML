MINIMIZE DELHI POLLUTION

• Promote Public Transport: Encourage use of the metro and public buses, and improve last-mile connectivity to reduce private vehicles. • Restrict Old Vehicles:Strictly enforce the ban on vehicles older than 10-15 years, which often have higher emissions. • Control Industrial Emissions:Ensure all factories use cleaner technologies and strictly monitor emissions, relocating highly polluting industries outside the city. • Manage Stubble Burning:Coordinate with neighboring states to provide farmers with alternatives to burning crop residue, such as Pusa Bio-decomposer or composting subsidies. • Ban Open Waste Burning:Enforce a complete ban on burning leaves and general waste, and improve municipal waste collection and management.
